How we work

We’ve chosen a measured, structured approach. Every collaboration relies on clear processes, autonomous stay management, and attentive local presence — for smooth rentals and shared peace of mind.

Our Collections

Three options, three distinct needs: manage rentals end-to-end, support you on the ground, or care for your home throughout the year. We guide you to the most fitting approach.

01

Serenity

Orchestrated, smooth, and fully controlled. We handle the on-site coordination: home preparation, turnovers, hotel-level standards, and stay follow-up — through established procedures and rigorous organisation.

This collection prioritises organisation, method, and tranquillity — not constant, scattered management.

02

Freedom

The essentials — tailored and perfectly organised. For owners who want to keep control of their bookings, while relying on a dependable, demanding local presence. Interventions are defined in advance, based on the home’s real needs and the rhythm of stays.

The Freedom Collection does not include booking management or daily guest messaging. It’s designed around autonomy and clear processes.

03

Private Stewardship

Caring for your home while you’re away. Planned visits, essential checks, and careful preparation before your arrival — so your home remains healthy, maintained, and ready to live in at any time.

Examples: control visits (airing, visual checks), occasional mail/delivery follow-up, “home opened” preparation before you arrive.

“Every home has its rhythm. Every owner, their expectations.”

We take the time to talk and define the best support — respecting your desired level of involvement, the home’s real use, and the expected standard.

Local signature

A special touch — when the moment calls for it

Some stays matter more than others: a proposal, a milestone birthday, or simply the desire to live something different for a few days.

Thanks to our local roots and deep knowledge of the area, we can — upon request — support these occasions with discretion, a sense of detail, and respect for the home.

  • Personalised recommendations (restaurants, discreet spots, local experiences)
  • Simple, elegant surprise arrangements
  • Occasional bookings (restaurants, taxis, local partners)
  • Suggestions adapted to the stay’s rhythm
These touches are offered case by case, in line with the stay and the owners’ wishes.
